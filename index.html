How to play

This game is about luck!

Objective of the game: Guess the total of two pair of hand.
Shown on screen, We got Opponent hand section, player hand section, player guess section, and Summary section.
Now with opponent and player hand , we got two pair of hands,
Each of hands are either show 5 (show of hand) or 0 (show of fist) only.

Step by step Guides
1. Choose what you decide to show on your both hand at Player hand sections.
2. Choose your guess range from 0, 5, 10, 15 and 20 at Player guess sections.
3. Sumamry sections shows your score, decisions and they are wait for you to Confirm!
4. Once comfirm, opponent hand will randomly show thier choice. 5. Player will get one point (wincount) once player guess right on the sum of total of four hands. Else, player will get a loosecount!
6. One game will have maximum 10 rounds. If player did not finish the 10 rounds, the remaining round will be counted as loose.

Best of luck!
